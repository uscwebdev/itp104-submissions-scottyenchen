Final Project Proposal

Topic

This is an online hub for sharing and discussing company interview experiences. Our platform, the "Interview Experience Hub", aims to assist job seekers by providing them with insights and discussions about interviews across various companies globally.

Audience

  • Students looking for jobs and preparing for interviews.
  • Individuals interested in sharing their personal interview experiences and tips.

Design & Style

The design will be professional and user-friendly, aiming to provide a straightforward experience for users.

Color Palette:

Primary Color
Secondary Color
Tertiary Color

Website Inspiration:

  • LinkedIn
  • Indeed
  • Glassdoor

Scope

The website will comprise four main pages:

  • Home: Introduction and recent interview experiences.
  • Interviews: A repository of interview experiences and questions.
  • Forum: A discussion platform for users.
  • Salary: A section where users can search and compare salaries for specific roles.
